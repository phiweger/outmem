# Q1 2026 product roadmap — internal sales reference

for several quarters:

- **Slack notifier** — outbound webhooks on threshold events.

Pricing: included in the standard Pro tier; no additional charge
for existing Pro customers. Marketing is preparing the launch
collateral; sales briefing scheduled the week of release.

No customer-specific commitments; no contract revisions. This is the
internal reference doc that the sales team will pin once launch is
announced publicly.
