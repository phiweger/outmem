Standard dosing for cats:

- **Ivermectin** — 0.2 mg/kg PO once for parasite prophylaxis. Do
  NOT use in collies or Australian shepherds (MDR1 sensitivity).
- **Amoxicillin** — 11 mg/kg PO BID for routine bacterial infections.
- **Metoclopramide** — 0.2-0.4 mg/kg PO TID for nausea.

Source: clinic formulary, section 3 (see provenance).
