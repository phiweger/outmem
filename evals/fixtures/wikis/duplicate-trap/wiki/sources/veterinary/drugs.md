# Clinic formulary — feline section

Section 3 — Antiparasitics
  Ivermectin: 0.2 mg/kg PO single dose for endoparasites.
  Contraindicated in MDR1-mutant breeds (collies, Aussies).

Section 4 — Antibiotics
  Amoxicillin: 11 mg/kg PO twice daily for routine infections.

Section 5 — Antiemetics
  Metoclopramide: 0.2 to 0.4 mg/kg PO three times daily.
