Acme Corp. is on a custom **cost-plus 30%** pricing arrangement for
the Initial Term (three years from the 2025-11 effective date).
Volume discounts from the standard schedule still apply.

This is the only active deviation from the [[pricing-formula]] —
every other customer is on cost-plus 35%. Acme reverts to the standard
formula on renewal; 90-day non-renewal notice is required.

Provider audit rights are limited to once per fiscal year on 30 days'
written notice (§11).
