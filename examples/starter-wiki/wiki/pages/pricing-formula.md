The 2026 pricing formula is **cost-plus 35%**, applied uniformly across
all SKUs. The +35% margin breaks down as:

- 12% direct support overhead
- 8% infrastructure amortisation
- 15% renewal reserve

This replaced the 2025 formula of cost-plus 30%, which had proven
insufficient to fund the renewal reserve.

Volume discounts (5% / 10% / 15% at $50k / $250k / $1M annual contract
value) compound with the +35% margin: margin first, then discount.

The only active exception is the Acme contract — see [[acme-msa]] for
the custom cost-plus 30% clause that runs for the Initial Term.
