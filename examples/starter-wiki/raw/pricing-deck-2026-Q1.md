# Pricing deck — Q1 2026 (extracted text)

## Slide 1 — Cover

"Outmem 2026 pricing — internal review draft."

## Slide 2 — Formula

The pricing formula for 2026 is **cost-plus 35%**, applied uniformly
across all SKUs. The +35% margin covers:

- Direct support overhead (~12%)
- Infrastructure amortisation (~8%)
- Reserve for renewals (~15%)

This replaces the 2025 formula of cost-plus 30%, which proved
insufficient to fund the renewal reserve.

## Slide 3 — Volume discounts

| Annual contract value | Discount |
|-----------------------|----------|
| <$50k                 | 0%       |
| $50k–$250k            | 5%       |
| $250k–$1M             | 10%      |
| >$1M                  | 15%      |

Volume discounts compound with the +35% margin (i.e. the margin is
applied first, then the discount is subtracted).

## Slide 4 — Exceptions

The Acme MSA (signed 2025-11) carries a custom clause: cost-plus 30%
for the duration of the initial 3-year term. This is the only
contract operating on the prior formula.
