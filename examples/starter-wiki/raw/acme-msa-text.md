# Acme Master Service Agreement — extract

## §4 — Pricing

Notwithstanding the standard pricing schedule, Customer (Acme Corp.)
shall be invoiced on a **cost-plus 30% basis** for all Services
rendered during the Initial Term (three years from the Effective Date).

For the avoidance of doubt: the standard cost-plus 35% formula does
not apply to Acme during the Initial Term. Volume discounts (see
standard schedule) apply.

## §7 — Renewal

Upon expiry of the Initial Term, pricing reverts to Provider's
then-current standard schedule. Acme has a 90-day notice period to
elect non-renewal.

## §11 — Audit rights

Customer may audit Provider's cost basis at most once per fiscal year
on 30 days' written notice.
